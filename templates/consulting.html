Objective

Create a order management system for warehouse workers to optimize the order fulfillment process

Background

Two other consultants and I were placed on a project to come up with a business case and prototype a concept to further streamline a standard warehouse management flow. Based on our research, we know that the existing process can be very manual and relies heavily on human judgment. It is usually a fast-paced environment with a lot of moving objects, which means it can be chaotic, dangerous and distracting. Finally, optimizing item pickup route requires experience and forward-thinking, and junior level workers may need additional guidance to become familiar with the warehouse surroundings.
