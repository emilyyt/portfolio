Selected Speaker

I was a selected speaker at GHC 2015 in Houston this past October for their Internet of Things, Emerging Technology segment. My topic was on the Improvement of Patient Experience within hospitals and over 1000 attendees were at my session!

UX Consulting
PricewaterhouseCoopers

In the past two years, I worked for PwC's UX team delivering apps to clients. One of my biggest projects was to develop new healthcare features for a large patient-facing mobile app. Some other platforms I've had a chance to dabble in include web, Google Glass, and beacons.

Eco-Touch
3D Touch Table Exhibit

For my senior HCI capstone at CMU, my team and I designed and prototyped a touch table exhibit with 3D game pieces that aims to teach the community environmental sustainable practices. We worked with the Stony Brook-Millstone Watershed Association.

Tamagotchi
Created with an Arduino

I had the chance to take a masters level HCI course on gadgets and sensors. The final project for the course was a self-defined Arduino creation. Tamagotchi is one of my favorite childhood toys and I decided to recreate it.

TSA Booth
Carnegie Mellon Annual Event

I was heavily involved in booth during my four years of college and was the vice president of TSA in my junior year. This is a large-scale project that student orgs take on annually to showcase their rendition of a well-known topic, usually derived from well-known cultures, movies, and games.
